Compress It is the ultimate solution for anyone who needs to compress images on the go. With just a few taps, you can easily compress multiple images and reduce their file size without compromising on quality.<br />

The latest version of Compress It comes with a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to select and compress multiple images at once. Simply tap on the 'Select Image' section on the top, choose the images you want to compress, and the app will show you the compressed images in a grid.<br />

You can easily compare the original and compressed size of each image by tapping on it. If you want to remove any image, simply hit the cross icon on the top right of the image and if you want you can even add more images<br />

When you are done just tap on the Share button on the bottom and share wherever you want
