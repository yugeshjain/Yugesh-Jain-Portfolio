Real-Time power grid conditions from the ERCOT region straight to your device!

The Electric Reliability Council of Texas (ERCOT) offers a free application for users in the Texas region to monitor grid conditions and operating reserves.

What's available:<br />
Informative welcome screens to walk new users through the app’s features<br />
ERCOT’s most popular dashboards have been added, expanded, and made interactive: Grid Conditions, Supply and Demand, Fuel Mix, and System-Wide (Wholesale) Prices<br />
Quick videos explaining dashboard features<br />
Personalization: Now customize which dashboards appear on the home screen<br />
Week-ahead meeting calendar view<br />
Enhanced notification settings: Opt in to receive TXANS and EEA alerts<br />
Dark mode<br />
Easy methods for submitting feedback<br />
